About Me

After completing a University degree in Internet & Business Technologies in 2011, I have worked in the public sector, private sector and worked for several design agencies over the years. In that time I have worked on projects from the ground up and I have been brought into projects to review the current state and implement changes for the future of a project. I have worked in agile teams and worked in sprint cycles for most of my professional career.

With an approach to leverage quantitative and qualititative data, I build the foundations on data and apply context of real world events and scenarios to deliver a data informed background.
